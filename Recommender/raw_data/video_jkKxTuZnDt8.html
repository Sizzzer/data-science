This is the part V of the Deep Learning Trading Strategy from the beginning to the production series.

In this video we are going to have a look at my fails. We will see what is wrong with highly imbalanced dataset, why ROC curve is not a good metric if your data set is imbalanced, and how the model performs in case of garbage data.

Note: As we are interested in profitable trades, we can replace all -1 and 255 labels with 0. After that we get binary labels and we can try to solve a binary classification task.
