Publicado em 1 de abr. de 2020

Water is one of the most important substances on Earth but in recent times, with the impact of climate change, it's critical to manage water supplies in a more effective manner.

Through the Google AI Impact Challenge, Hadi and the team at The American University of Beirut have received support from Google.org to build a smartphone app for farmers that uses machine learning to help farmers become more efficient with existing water resources.

Learn more about the Google AI Impact Challenge - https://g.co/AIChallenge and The American University of Beirut - https://www.aub.edu.lb/
