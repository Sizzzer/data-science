Publicado em 20 de abr. de 2020

We at Develandoo have recently announced that our Meetup group ''Power Humanity with AI'' is going Global & Virtual. It means in the upcoming months, Develandoo will be organizing and running a series of webinars and virtual online sessions which will include leading professionals in the field of AI and Machine Learning.
This is the first of a series of webinars organized by our Meetup group. The webinar is designed for those who are eager to learn and exchange knowledge and have some insightful discussions around Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science.
We decided to present short pieces from each of our webinars for you to more easily digest the topic.
The webinar has been conducted by our Lead Data Scientist Armen Ghambaryan.

Armen has been developing analytical systems based on state-of-the-art machine learning models for banking, healthcare and media industries for more than 4 years. He holds a Ph.D. degree in Economics. He is a co-founder of Protogen AI which provides credit risk scoring services for financial institutions. Armen is also lecturing at the American University of Armenia.
